About Restored Masonry

Preserving Ottawa's architectural heritage with craftsmanship and care

Our Story

Founded in 2004, Restored Masonry has been dedicated to the preservation and restoration of Ottawa's historic buildings. With over 20 years of experience, we specialize in heritage masonry work for structures built before 1950.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern building pathology expertise to ensure that historic buildings are not only preserved but strengthened for future generations.

Our Expertise

Heritage Conservation

We use traditional materials and techniques that respect the historical integrity of each building.

Building Pathology

Advanced diagnostic techniques to identify and address structural issues before they become major problems.

Material Science

Deep knowledge of historic building materials and their proper restoration methods.

Regulatory Compliance

Expertise in navigating heritage designation requirements and local building codes.

Our Commitment

At Restored Masonry, we believe that preserving historic buildings is not just about maintaining structures—it's about safeguarding our community's history and cultural identity. Every project is approached with the utmost respect for the building's original design and materials.

We work closely with heritage committees, architects, and property owners to ensure that our restorations meet the highest standards of authenticity and durability.
