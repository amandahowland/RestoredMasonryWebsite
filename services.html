Our Services

Comprehensive masonry restoration and repair services for historic buildings

Repointing & Tuckpointing

Traditional repointing using authentic lime-based mortars that allow historic brickwork to breathe and move naturally. We carefully match original mortar compositions and colors.

  • Lime mortar repointing
  • Historic mortar analysis
  • Color matching
  • Weather protection

Brick & Stone Restoration

Expert repair and replacement of damaged or deteriorated brick and stone elements. We source matching materials and use traditional cutting and shaping techniques.

  • Brick replacement
  • Stone carving and repair
  • Material sourcing
  • Structural reinforcement

Cement Removal

Removal of inappropriate Portland cement mortars that trap moisture and cause deterioration. We replace with breathable lime mortars for long-term preservation.

  • Portland cement removal
  • Surface preparation
  • Lime mortar replacement
  • Moisture management

Lintel & Arch Repair

Restoration of structural lintels and decorative arches using traditional materials and techniques. We ensure structural integrity while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

  • Steel lintel replacement
  • Arch reconstruction
  • Load-bearing assessment
  • Decorative restoration

Foundation & Chimney Work

Structural repairs to foundations and chimneys, addressing settlement, cracks, and deterioration. We use appropriate materials for historic structures.

  • Foundation stabilization
  • Chimney reconstruction
  • Crack repair
  • Waterproofing

Building Pathology Assessment

Comprehensive diagnostic services to identify causes of deterioration and recommend appropriate repairs. Includes moisture analysis and structural evaluation.

  • Moisture testing
  • Structural assessment
  • Material analysis
  • Repair recommendations
